About Malawi Forecasts

Malawi Forecasts is an early warning prediction system designed to provide accurate and timely climate forecasts for Malawi. Our goal is to empower communities, organizations, and decision-makers with actionable insights to anticipate and respond to climate-related challenges.

  • Leverages advanced data analytics and machine learning
  • Supports resilience and preparedness for climate variability
  • Accessible and user-friendly for all Malawians

By using this platform, you contribute to a more informed and resilient Malawi.
